Lessons On The Lake Music Arts Academy is the premier music school in Mooresville, NC and The Lake Norman area. Students enroll from all around the Metro-Charlotte and Lake Norman areas. We offer quality lessons for all ages! We Look forward to having you or your child apart of the Lessons On The Lake family!

Policies

Payments

Lesson payment is due the the 1st of the month. Teachers accept check or cash. If paying by check, please make it out to the instructor. You can also choose to pay for lessons through online platforms

Missed Lessons

Each instructor has their own policy on missed lessons. For questions, please check with your instructor.

Calendar

For a list of events and programs, please visit our calendar page on our web site.

Phone Calls to Instructors

Please feel free to call your instructor for any messages you need to convey.

Snow and Bad Weather

Please contact your instructor on days where there is inclement weather to check and see if lessons are being offered.

Background Checks

All instructors, interns and board members are required to have a background checks.

Cameras

For the safety of our students and instructors, all rooms at HMF have cameras installed in them.
